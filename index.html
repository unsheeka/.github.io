About Me

My name is Anshika, and I have been an active linguist for the past six years, where 5 years of teaching encapsulated the essence of my methodologies in teaching. I have made a shift from being an educator to contributing in the field of AI. As a linguistic consultant I have been responsible for transliteration of the text from my mother tongue to English, creating phonological connections through audio or text files. This also involves data annotation and transcription of the text. I believe I bring a perfect blend of EFL and technological skills.
