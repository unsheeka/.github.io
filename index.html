About Me

My name is Daisy and I am a linguist working on Meta's Fundamental AI Research Team (FAIR).

I have a background in linguistics and computer science; I enjoy working on projects with a focus on semantics, sociolinguistics, and human-computer interaction (HCI).

What I'm doing

  • Meta

    I am currently working on Meta's Massively Multilingual Speech (MMS) project that aims to support under-resourced languages using AI and self-supervised learning.

  • Linguistic Consulting

    I am a freelance linguistic consultant with Productive Playhouse, an international production and language company based in Los Angeles.
